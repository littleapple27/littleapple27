About Me

An apple symbolizes lifelong learning and healthy living; both are personal mantras. My son's last name translates as little apple from Italian and is the apple of my eye. Coincidentally, I work on a 13.5" Apple MacBook Pro, and an apple is a perfect energy boost on long hikes.

I am a mother, web developer, hiker, birder, schooner enthusiast, and a lifelong learner. My favorite drink is Kombucha, and my signature dish is Chicken Tortilla Soup. I appreciate long drives down the road less traveled and sailing upon the sea with my significant other, a licensed captain. I have scuba dived to depths of 90' below the ocean's surface and hiked to peaks that reach more than 14,000' above sea level. The combination of an active mind and an active body is my holy grail.
